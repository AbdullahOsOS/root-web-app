Every connection must be metered as it comes to the electricity meters.Home owners have the option of installing either a regular or a smart meter. Electricity meters are devices for electricity consumption measurements which have been used and are still in use today with modern technologies.

Smart Meter helps in:

Smart metering helps electricity consumers:

- Adjust their habits and lower electricity bills.


- Obtain more detailed feedback on electricity usage.


- Optimize income with existing resources.


- Dynamic pricing, raises or lowers the cost of electricity.


- Reduce the number of system-wide electricity failures

Benefits of smart meters

Smart meters are improved versions of conventional power meters that are developed after AMR and AMI improvements.

In addition to metering features,smart meters are capable to calculate several parameters as power factor and to predict power consumption at particular intervals.

it also allows users to remote monitoring and remote control for their home energy management systems.
